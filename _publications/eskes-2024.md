## Abstract
This technical note evaluates the performance of the Copernicus Atmosphere Monitoring Service (CAMS) Cy48R1 upgrade implemented in June 2023, assessing improvements in atmospheric composition forecasting and analysis across multiple chemical species and atmospheric parameters.
